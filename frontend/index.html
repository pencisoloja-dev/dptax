Frequently Asked Questions

When is the filing deadline?

The usual deadline for filing the federal return is April 15. If you need more time, you can request an extension before that date.

What happens if I don't file my return on time?

You could receive penalties or late interest. We help you file even if the deadline has passed or if you need to catch up on previous years.

What documents do I need to file my taxes?

It depends on your situation, but typically you need: W-2 form (employees) or 1099 (independent contractors), personal identification (SSN or ITIN), proof of income and expenses, and dependent information if applicable.

Can I file if I don't have a Social Security Number (SSN)?

Yes. We can help you obtain and use an ITIN (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number) to meet your tax obligations.

Do you accept scanned documents or photos?

Yes. You can upload them directly from your phone or computer in a secure and encrypted manner.

Is it safe to share my information online?

Yes. We use SSL/TLS encryption, secure servers, and privacy protocols aligned with IRS standards. Your information is confidential and protected.

Is my information shared with other companies?

No. Your data is used solely to prepare and file your tax return.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

The IRS usually processes refunds within 7 to 21 days if the return is filed electronically.

How will I receive my refund?

You can choose direct deposit, physical check, or a prepaid debit card, depending on your preference.

What if I owe taxes?

We can help you set up a payment plan or make the payment directly to the IRS from your bank account.

How much does the service cost?

The cost depends on the complexity of your return. We offer a free evaluation before starting the process.

Do you serve independent workers or business owners?

Yes. We prepare returns for employees, independent contractors, small businesses, and self-employed workers.

Can I file my return completely online?

Yes. The entire process can be done virtually, from document submission to electronic signature.

Can I schedule an in-person or video call appointment?

Of course. We offer in-person and virtual appointments based on your availability.

What do I do if I can't file before April 15?

We can help you request an extension until October 15, avoiding late filing penalties.

Can you help me with past due returns?

Yes. We can file your returns from previous years and help you get current with the IRS.

Can I file if I live outside the United States?

Yes. We assist residents and non-residents with US income, including expatriate cases.

Do you help students or first-time filers?

Yes. We guide you step by step so your first return is simple and correct.
